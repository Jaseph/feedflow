FeedFlow - Free Open Source RSS Reader

FeedFlow is a modern, self-hosted RSS and Atom feed reader. It's completely free, open source, and privacy-friendly with no tracking.

Features

  • Free and open source RSS reader
  • Self-hosted feed aggregator
  • No database required
  • Privacy-friendly - no tracking
  • Dark mode and light mode
  • Mobile responsive design
  • OPML import and export
  • Smart feed caching
  • RSS and Atom support
  • Search articles
  • Organize feeds by category
